Winter Training Program

For All students undergoing B.Tech /M.Tech/ B.E / MCA/ M.Sc. IT/ B.Sc.IT/ BCA / B-VOC/ PGDCA and any other 1 year diploma in computers/ professionals etc.

Professionals & Computer Trainers, who are interested in upgrading their skills in a fast track program.


The training is conducted in the months of Dec & Jan every year. We start admissions for the winter training program from the month of October every year. Depending upon the college schedule students either get a break of 15 days of 30 days.We have designed special fast track courses for the same where we complete the training program during above mentioned duration. We conduct separate batches for students as well as professionals both.



Summer Training Program

For All students undergoing B.Tech /M.Tech/ B.E / MCA/ M.Sc. IT/ B.Sc.IT/ BCA / B-VOC/ PGDCA and any other 1 year diploma in computers/ professionals etc.

Passout students who want to fill the gap between their skills and the skills required at the Industry.

Students after B.Tech 6th semester year can undergo this program to do Industrial Training, as a mandatory requirement after completing their 3rd year. We help them prepare their summer training report for the same. Students develop a summer project as part of the course.


The training is conducted in the months of May, June & July. We start admissions for the summer training program from the month of March every year. We provide training with only one objective in mind – how to reduce the skill gap between the students skills and the industry requirements. We feel this is the most important period because without any tension of college classes with total dedication during this period you actually learn skills important for getting a job.



Final Year Project Training Program/ Internship Program/ Live Project Training Programs

For All students undergoing B.Tech / MCA/ M.Sc. IT/ B.Sc.IT/ BCA etc.

Mandatory requirement for B.Tech/ MCA/ BCA final year Students.

Project training programs are conducted twice a year for the final year students. Either these programs start from the month of Dec / Jan or from July/ Aug. For the mandatory project of final year, we start admissions from the month of October every year. Other students have a choice to undergo these programs either from winter session or from summer session.
